PACER Fee Structure Update: What Changed and How to Minimize Cost in High-Volume Searches

PACER's updated fee structure takes effect June 2026. For investigators running large-scale bankruptcy docket searches, this briefing explains the cost implications and alternatives.

April 2026 3-minute read Locke & Boole Analyst Team

PACER's updated fee structure takes effect June 2026. For investigators running large-scale bankruptcy docket searches, the new pricing has meaningful cost implications. Practical guidance on batching and alternatives.

The pricing change

The updated structure increases per-page costs for some document types while leaving others unchanged. This affects high-volume searches for bankruptcy dockets and related filings.

Cost mitigation

Investigators should batch requests, prioritize key documents, and use summary docket reports when available to reduce page-by-page fees.

Process update

Review current docket retrieval workflows and shift lower-value searches to alternative public record sources when possible.
