In complex litigation, high-stakes M&A, and cross-border compliance, we strip away the layers of shell entities, holding companies, and nominee directors to reveal the ultimate beneficial owners.

Our advantage is structural monitoring. We do not simply generate a report and leave. We provide dynamic mapping and continuous integrity alerts to ensure your partners remain compliant — and your structure remains resilient.

Evidence, Not Guesswork

Every finding independently
verifiable. No exceptions.

Locke & Boole operates on a strict "data first" philosophy. We do not rely on proprietary databases — we rely on the public record. Every finding we produce can be independently verified using the same public sources we cite.
01
Entity Identification
We begin with Secretary of State filings, registered agent records, and annual reports across all relevant jurisdictions, constructing the initial corporate family tree.
02
Financial Threading
EDGAR filings, federal procurement registries, and bankruptcy dockets are cross-referenced against UCC liens to surface financial interdependencies.
03
Physical Verification
County tax assessor databases and geospatial imagery confirm the physical existence of claimed assets and link entities to real-world addresses.
04
Structural Mapping
All data threads are synthesized into a visual ownership chain with footnoted citations for every claim — formatted for subpoena-ready delivery.
05
Continuous Monitoring
For retainer clients, we maintain live surveillance of your monitored entities and deliver automated alerts on registered agent changes, dissolutions, and new encumbrances.
